# Product Hiring Exploration

**Started:** February 23, 2026

## What This Space Is
This folder is an extension of the `personal-reflections` process. It is dedicated to observing and tracking a specific "pull" or curiosity: **building a single-person-driven offering around deep, 1:1 matching in product hiring.**

It is NOT a business plan. It is NOT an execution roadmap (yet).
It is a sandbox to explore this idea from the Grounded Action (5% clarity) mindset discussed in the November 2025 reflection.

## The Core Investigation
From the initial reflection (Feb 2026):
> *"I think I really like to work one-on-one with people so I could really help in the hiring challenges."*

This is the anchor.
- The objective isn't to start a recruiting agency.
- The objective is to see what happens when the natural flow ("what gives energy: 1:1 discussions, figuring yourself out") is applied to a specific, broken ecosystem (product domain hiring) in an authentic, outcome-detached manner.

## Rules of Exploration Here
1. **No forced scale:** Keep the thinking focused on a one-person, completely independent operation ("answerable to no one per se").
2. **Observe the energy:** Only proceed with explorations that give energy. If something brings anxiety, recognize it and write it down.
3. **Log truth:** Use `EVOLUTION.md` to document conversations, epiphanies, confusions, and moments of clarity. Date everything. Don't hide the messy thoughts.

## Initial Themes / Problems to Unpack
- **The Information Asymmetry:** JDs vs Reality. 
- **The Reality of "Urgency":** When are companies *actually* looking to hire right away? 
- **The Candidate's Burden:** What do candidates really want, and are they even an A-player for *that specific context*?
- **Domain Focus:** Product Design, Product Manager, Product Data.
